How to Submit Assignments¶

The process for submitting your work is always the same:

  1. Finish the assignment.
  2. commit and push your work to a repository on your GitHub profile.
  3. Find the corresponding Canvas assignment on launchcode.instructure.com and enter the GitHub URL for your project.
  4. Find a TA and demo your project for them.
